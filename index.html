Hyped Goods

Hyped Goods is a fully functional e-commerce website created to market trendy sneakers in high demand. The webpage design capitalizes on promoting a user-friendly online shopping experience. Some of the administration accessibility features the ability to add/remove sale products, edit products, administer users, and oversee all customer orders. Users are able to browse products, add products to their cart, checkout their order, and see the records of their previous orders.

Sqratch Xchange

Sqratch Xchange Website is built and designed for a thrift store business to help the owner showcase their items online and to bring more customers to their shop. It’s built with capability for the admin to create/delete/update items. Users can also send a message to the admin to inquire about any items.

Fire House Restaurant

The MovieDB

The Movie DB is a web app that gives users the accessibility to search their favorite films and discover some new movies at the palm of their hands. It provides movie overview, casting information, trailers, images, and movie suggestions.

Vape3one E-commerce Website

Vape3one is an e-commerce website for vaping necessities. It’s designed to help the business create more traffic and generate more sales online.

Hoop Finder

Hoop Finder is a website that allows users to easily discover nearby locations basketball courts. Users are able to leave a court review and player review, which is view-able by any visitors on the website. Users can add other users to be their friends. The site is also built in with a check in functionality to show how many users are currently playing in respective courts. Users can also set events to let other users know that the court is going to be busy at the set time.
